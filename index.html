FAQs

  • How do I Login?

    1. Log on to https://www.wecare.com
    2. Always remember your login credentials- user name and password, as you will need it every time you access the platform. Your username will be provided to you during the onboarding process. You have the option to change your password and make sure to make one that will be easy for you to remember and would be hard for others to guess.

  • How frequently is the information in the website updated?

    The information in our website is updated every time there is a new diagnosis for the patient.

  • I just want to talk to a doctor. Is this really necessary?

    That is your decision, however, remember one of the top 3-5 major cause of death in most cases is medical errors due to the lack of accurate information regarding older prescriptions. Therefore I would suggest you to use our website.

  • Is there any fee associated with using the website or accessing my medical records

    There is no fee associated with accessing your medical records from the website.
